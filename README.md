## English Guide

### ❌ Common error messages
You may see one of these at checkout:
* `Your credit card was declined.`
* `Payment was not approved. Please try another card.`
* `Unusual activity has been detected. Please try again later.`
* A verification loop that never completes.

### 🔍 Why a legitimate card still gets declined
Most declines are **not** about having no money. The common, fixable causes are:

| Cause | What's happening |
| :--- | :--- |
| International / recurring payments disabled | Your bank blocks cross-border or subscription (recurring) online charges by default. |
| Card type restricted | Some prepaid or virtual cards are not accepted by the provider. |
| Bank fraud hold (false positive) | A first-time foreign SaaS charge can trigger a temporary hold your bank must release. |
| Details mismatch | A typo in card number, expiry, CVC, or billing address. |
| 3DS authentication dropped | Aggressive ad-blockers can break the bank's 3-D Secure popup. |

### 🛠️ How to fix it (the legitimate way)
1. **Double-check the details.** Card number, expiry, CVC, and **billing address** must match your bank's records exactly.
2. **Call your card issuer.** Ask them to (a) enable **international online payments**, (b) allow **recurring/subscription** charges, and (c) release any temporary fraud hold on the attempted charge. This solves the majority of cases.
3. **Confirm funds & limits.** Make sure the card has available balance and you haven't hit a per-transaction or daily limit.
4. **Use a mainstream card.** A standard credit/debit card from a major bank works far more reliably than a restricted prepaid/virtual card.
5. **Disable ad-blockers, then retry in a private window** so the 3-D Secure verification can load.
6. **Try the official mobile app's in-app purchase** (Apple App Store / Google Play) as an alternative billing route.
7. **Still stuck?** Contact the provider's billing support with a screenshot of the exact error.

## 中文排查指南

### ❌ 常见报错

### 🔍 正规卡为什么也会被拒
多数被拒**不是没钱**,而是下面这些常见、可解决的原因:

| 原因 | 具体情况 |
| :--- | :--- |
| 未开通境外/订阅扣款 | 银行默认拦截跨境或周期性(订阅)在线扣款。 |
| 卡类型受限 | 部分预付卡、虚拟卡不被官方接受。 |
| 银行风控误拦 | 首次境外 SaaS 扣款可能被临时冻结,需银行放行。 |
| 信息不符 | 卡号、有效期、CVC 或账单地址填错。 |
| 3DS 验证中断 | 广告拦截插件可能让银行 3DS 弹窗加载失败。 |

### 🛠️ 正规解决步骤
1. **核对信息**:卡号、有效期、CVC、**账单地址**要和银行登记的完全一致。
2. **打发卡行电话**:请客服(a)开通**境外在线支付**、(b)允许**订阅/周期扣款**、(c)放行刚才那笔的临时风控冻结。多数情况这一步就解决了。
3. **确认余额与限额**:确保有可用余额,且没触发单笔/单日限额。
4. **换正规卡**:大银行的标准信用卡/借记卡,比受限的预付卡、虚拟卡稳得多。
5. **关掉广告拦截插件,无痕窗口重试**,让 3DS 验证能正常加载。
6. **改走官方 App 内购**(App Store / Google Play)作为备用扣款通道。
7. **仍不行**:截图保留报错,联系官方账单客服。
